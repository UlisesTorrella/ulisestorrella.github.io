## Daily Teutuli:
The teutuli championship is a F1 style championship, where players play daily geoguessr challenges and try to outperform their rivals in the weekly standings (GPs).
At the end of the week, the Grand Prix results award points for the Championship following the [F1 scoring system](https://racingnews365.com/f1-points-system).

Daily challenges are sent by email every day at 8am (CET) along with the current standings. All my friends and friends of my friends can participate by joining [this mailing list](https://groups.google.com/g/daily-teutulis).
